About Shivtej

I MAKE
FOOD LOOK
IRRESISTIBLE

Hi, I'm Shivtej — a videographer and creative editor with a passion for food, storytelling, and the art of the perfect cut.

I specialise in short-form video ads for cafes and restaurants. My work isn't just about filming food — it's about capturing the atmosphere, the warmth, and the craving that makes someone put down their phone and walk through your door.

From the first sip of morning coffee to the last bite of a late-night dessert, I tell your brand's story through cinematic visuals and precise, rhythmic editing that's built for social media and built to convert.
